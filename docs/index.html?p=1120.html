10/12 – THE HASH FOR RED OCTOBER

Posted on October 2, 2013 by admin

When : 10/12 Saturday 3pm

Where :  Yellowwood State Forest

https://maps.google.com/maps?q=39.192015,-86.345416

Hares :

  • GoesinTite
  • Fiddler on the Poop
  • Screw It, I’m Wet
  • Souzie Bee Stroker

What do you get when you combine the gorgeous fall leaves, a recently departed best-selling Cold War-era author, and an unusually high percentage of gingers just itching to hare for you, some of whom long for the simpler days of the Cold War?

THE HASH FOR RED OCTOBER!

Prepare to have your baffles cleared as we Crazy Ivan all over Yellowwood Forest this Saturday, October 12, 2013 at 3:00 p.m. TIME CHANGE! TIME CHANGE! TIME CHANGE! TIME CHANGE!

You don’t have to wear red, but the Yankee Doodlers out there might catch sound of you if you don’t. One ping. That’s all it takes. You have been warned!

Your hares will be Screw it I’m Wet, Fiddler on the Poop, Souzie Bee Stroker, Goesentite, and an assortment of gingers to be named later.

There will be beer, orange food, red food, and perhaps even a special concoction of some sort along the shitty, shiggyful trail. All at 3:00pm! TIME CHANGE! TIME CHANGE! tIME CHANGE!

The cost for comrades is a meager $6.00, new recruits get in free.

The turnoff to Yellowwood State Forest is off of 46 E, between Bloomington and Nashville. Cumming from Bloomington, you’ll want to turn left off of 46 onto Jackson Creek Road, then left onto Yellowwood Road. Stay on Yellowwood Road until you get to the parking area (at Extraction Point A) on the north end of the lake.

Until Saturday (AT 3:00pm!!!!), do svidanya!
